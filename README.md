## What Actually Makes Them Stand Out

### 1. The Pricing Is What It Says It Is

Most hosting companies advertise low introductory rates that balloon on renewal. iFastNet doesn't do that. Multiple long-term customers specifically mention this in their reviews. One user from the UK wrote that he'd been on a monthly Standard account for about a year and a half, and appreciated that "you pay $4.99 every month, exactly as advertised." That's rarer than you'd think.

### 2. NVMe-Powered Servers

Their premium shared hosting plans run on NVMe storage — not just standard SSDs. NVMe drives are significantly faster than traditional SSDs because they communicate via PCIe rather than the older SATA interface. For a hosting provider at this price point, that's a legitimate differentiator. One customer noted that websites load up to 1000% faster on their NVMe hosting compared to older storage types.

### 3. Free SSL on Every Plan

All plans include free SSL certificates via Let's Encrypt. You can issue certificates to every domain in your account. For context, there are still UK web hosts in 2026 that charge extra for this. iFastNet includes it across the board.

### 4. Cloudflare Integration Built In

Every hosting plan comes with Cloudflare integration activated through cPanel — one click. Cloudflare adds a CDN layer, improves load speeds globally, and helps protect against DDoS attacks and bad bots. Most hosts charge for this as an add-on or make you set it up manually.

### 5. cPanel + Softaculous

iFastNet uses the industry-standard cPanel interface, which means if you've ever used any mainstream hosting before, you'll be right at home. Softaculous is included with all plans and supports 240+ one-click app installs — WordPress, Joomla, Magento, PrestaShop, OpenCart, and many more.
